About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Economics at York University specializing in Econometrics. My research explores the Canadian digital divide from both individual and organizational perspectives. At the individual level, I study the determinants of internet and financial technology usage across Canada. At the organizational level, I examine how digital and financial technologies impact Canadian business efficiency and cybersecurity.

Research Interests

  • Econometrics
  • Survey Analysis
  • Applied Econometrics
  • Financial Technologies
  • Business Cybersecurity and Technological Efficiency
